-FAQ-

  1. What currencies does Country Wide Tours and Travels exchange?
    Country Wide Tours and Travels deals in a wide range of foreign currencies, including but not limited to USD, EUR, GBP, AUD, CAD, and many others. Please contact us for specific currency inquiries.
  2. What are your exchange rates?
    Our exchange rates vary based on market conditions and the currency you wish to exchange. For the most up-to-date rates, please visit our website or contact our customer service team.
  3. Do I need to provide any documents for currency exchange?
    Yes, you need to provide a valid government-issued ID, such as a passport, Aadhar card, or driver’s license. Additional documentation may be required depending on the amount and type of currency being exchanged.
  4. Is there a limit on the amount of currency I can exchange?
    The limit on currency exchange depends on various factors, including regulatory requirements. Please contact us for specific details regarding your transaction.
  5. Can I reserve a specific currency in advance?
    Yes, you can pre-book your currency online or over the phone to ensure availability when you visit our branch.
  6. Do you offer doorstep delivery services?
    Yes, Country Wide Tours and Travels offers doorstep delivery of foreign currency within Bangalore. Please check with our team for service availability and any associated charges.
  7. What payment methods do you accept for currency exchange?
    We accept payments via cash, debit/credit cards, and bank transfers. Please note that certain payment methods may incur additional fees.
  8. How long does the currency exchange process take?
    The currency exchange process is typically quick, taking only a few minutes once all required documents are provided. Larger transactions may take slightly longer.
  9. Do you provide travel cards or forex cards?
    Yes, Country Wide Tours and Travels offers travel cards/forex cards that are a convenient and secure way to carry multiple foreign currencies while traveling.
  10. What are your business hours?
    Our business hours are from MON to SAT to 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday through Saturday. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays. Please check our website for any special holiday hours.
