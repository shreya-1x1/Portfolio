Design Handbook for Storages


Explore options suitable to your space and lifestyle

Types of Storages

With the use of one can create in simple modules finite design options suitable to any space/ corner o the house.

TV Unit

Serving as a focal point for your room, a TV unit is a must-have. From wall-mounted designs to free-standing units clubbed with open shelves, they provide additional storage space.

Shoe Cabinet

An essential unit for a clutter-free and well-organised space right at the entrance. A shoe rack helps keep your shoes intact and can be clubbed with a bench to add more functionality.

Bar Unit

Give a classy edge to a hall/ dining space with various assortments to store your glasses, ice and bottle of the quencher. A bar unit can be planned by combing multiple shutter options along with mechanisms to facilitate storing and displaying.

CORE MATETIALS

MR Plywood

Moisture Resistant plywood is well known for its strength and utility. Commonly used in hot and humid conditions, mainly in tropical areas for furniture which are not vulnerable to water spillage.

BWR Plywood

Boiling water-resistant plywood, is highly water-resistant plywood. Made from phenolic resins, it has high resistance to water is widely used to make furniture for home, office as well as outdoors.

BWP Plywood

Boiling Water Proof plywood is known to withstand prolonged exposure to water and moisture. This grade is known for its uniform thickness and high load-bearing capacity.

HDHMR

High-Density High Moisture Resistance or HDHMR plywood produced by mixing and pressing forest wood waste and fibre chips is an economic alternative of High-grade plywood since they are extremely water-resistant and have uniform thickness.

SURFACE FINISHES

Laminate

It is a multi-layer synthetic wood product. It is created of high-density fibre, melamine resin or wood particles. They come with multiple options like suede, gloss, wooden and textured.

Membrane

It is basically a Polyvinyl chloride (PVC) foil which is wrapped around MDF (medium- density fibreboard) under high pressure. It is a good alternative to laminates and have similar options like wooden and gloss.

Lacquered Glass

Also known as "Back painted glass", this finish gives any shutters a premium look. Available in multiple solid colours, they are now proved to be one of the most popular finishes.

Acrylic

Made of polymers, this finish lends a glossy finish and has a seamless look. Very popular for kitchen shutters due to its reflective mirror- like finish and is a good alternative to lacquered glass.

Add-ons...

Drawer

Drawer systems are space effective and can be clubbed with mats and trays to organise the space. Choosing a soft-close mechanism is preferable as they enable the slide to close quietly, even when slammed shut.

Pocket Door Mechanism

This mechanism enables a door to slide in and out of a pocket. When employed in a unit, the door does not need a clear place to swing nor does it restrict access to the inside space. Best suitable for puja units, walk- ins, etc.

Lift -Up /Down

Add dynamism to furniture by using a lift-up/ down mechanism which enables better space utility. Mostly used in study/ laundry table.

Lights

Add functionality and aesthetics to any storage unit by adding lights. With a wide range to choose from, LED strip lights with an added sensor option are the most common in use.<
