ANTRA –A FAMILY RESTAURANT

Antra Veg Restaurant offers a royal ambience complemented by a lavish spread of pure vegetarian cuisine, perfect for family outings or delightful dining escapades. With a princely setup and soothing lighting, Antra provides an array of South to North Indian delicacies, Continental, and Chinese dishes. With 100 seats available, guests can even inspect the finest kitchen. Antra ensures a separate veg kitchen, live visuals, and impeccable service, making it a haven for vegans.
