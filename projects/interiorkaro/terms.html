2. PROJECT AND ORDER:

The term "project" shall herein refer to the comprehensive and diligent endeavour of conceptualising, planning, and executing the design and construction of a singular residential dwelling. In the context of a project, it is permissible to initiate multiple orders, including but not limited to an order for modular products and a distinct order for services pertaining to civil work. Payments shall be processed in accordance with the terms and conditions set forth in this agreement, on a per-order basis.

3. REFUND OF BOOKING AMOUNT:

Upon the execution of the project booking, a period of seventy-two (72) consecutive hours shall be granted, herein referred to as the "free look window," within which you, the party of the first part, shall possess the absolute discretion to exercise your right to terminate the aforementioned project. Upon the expiration of the aforementioned time period, it shall be explicitly understood that no reimbursements or monetary compensations shall be granted in the event of project termination.

Notwithstanding any other provisions stated herein, Interiorkaro hereby asserts its prerogative to exercise the authority to subtract a sum equivalent to 25% of the total booking fee, exclusive of any applicable taxes, from the aforementioned booking fee. Said deduction shall be allocated towards the remuneration for design and consultation services rendered by Interiorkaro.

4. BOOKING FEE:

The aforementioned booking fee, amounting to 10% of the payment, as stated in the tentative quote, shall be duly acknowledged as a credit towards the work order currently under discussion with the client.

5. EXECUTION STAGES:

The execution stages are used to track the order's progress. They signify the achievement of significant milestones, at which point the remaining balance payment (specific to that order type) is due. Interiorkaro will complete the remainder of the order only after receiving payment for the respective execution stage.

6. ESTIMATED PROJECT VALUE/QUOTE:

The quote provided is a preliminary estimate based on your specifications. The final estimate may be affected by variables such as the condition of the site, the design, the materials and the services, and is therefore subject to change.

Any substantial change to the project's scope or value may result in a modification to the discount or offer associated with the booking fee.

7. QUOTE VALIDITY:

The quotes given during the pre-booking stage remain valid for a duration of 16 days. If the booking is confirmed after this period, the prices may be revised.

Upon the reception of the booking amount, the specified material costing in the quote will remain valid for a duration of 60 days. In the event of any delay in the project plan resulting in a production phase exceeding 60 days, the quote value will be modified to correspond with the updated material costing.

8. DELIVERY ASSURANCE:

We assure you that your order will be delivered by the agreed-upon delivery date, unless any changes are made to the order or scope of work after confirmation, or due to circumstances beyond the control of Interiorkaro. These factors encompass events of force majeure and modifications in the relevant legislation.

9. MODULAR DELIVERY:

In instances wherein the scope of Interiorkaro's involvement solely pertains to modular products, it is hereby requested that the customer provide confirmation of the material delivery date in accordance with the site's preparedness, specifically with regards to the completion of civil work. Upon completion of on-site services and receipt of final payment, the material shall be dispatched. In the event that the aforementioned site is not prepared for the intended purpose, it is hereby stipulated that the materials shall be retained within the confines of the designated factory for a period not exceeding five days subsequent to the originally scheduled date of delivery. Subsequent to said three-day period, it is further specified that charges for storage shall be imposed at a rate of Rs 500 per day until such time as the materials are officially dispatched.

10. DELAY PENALTIES:

Interiorkaro shall assume full liability for the payment of a delay penalty amounting to INR 500 per day in the event of a delay in the handover of the project exceeding 15 working days, excluding Sundays and Public Holidays, from the confirmed handover date. The stipulated date of transfer is duly communicated within the Client Service Agreement; nevertheless, it is contingent upon the client's adherence to the prescribed payment schedule and the generation of all sales orders pertaining to the project.

1. The imposition of a delay penalty shall be contingent upon the complete fulfilment of all the prescribed norms in accordance with the Interiorkaro Guarantee.

2. The party responsible for any delay shall be liable for a penalty, herein referred to as the "maximum delay penalty," which shall not exceed three percent (3%) of the total value of the project.

3. The penalty for delay shall be disbursed by means of a reimbursement to the customer's designated bank account, on the condition that the client has fulfilled their obligation of remitting 100% of the Project Amount in accordance with the predetermined schedule and upon the Completion of Handover.
