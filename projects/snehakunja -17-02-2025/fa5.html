    Hospital (Vivekananda Arogyadhama):

    Snehakunja has a well-equipped 20 bed-hospital at Kasarkod with two ayurvedic doctors practicing Ayurveda and Panchakarma line of treatment. Vivekanada Aarogyadhama (ayurvedic hospital), provides general health services and also treatments for a few critical illnesses. We have special, semi special and general wards to cater to the specific needs of the patients. The patients from lower income groups are offered free consultation and concessional medicines. We also conduct free medical checkup and dispense medicines to poor patients once a week.

    Outpatient Department

    Vivekananda Aarogya Dhama provides medical and treatment services to out-patients between 7.30AM to 6.30 PM. The daycare patients could consult the AYUSH specialists (with prior appointment) for their general and critical health problems. Some of the special treatments such as Panchakarma, Abhyanga Chikitsa, Dhara Chikitsa, Poorvakarma and Paschatkarma of Panchakarma are also conducted on daycare basis.

    Cottages for guests and patients:

    Snehakunja has 4 twin-cottages with two bed rooms each with wash room facilities can accommodate 10-12 patients or guests. The visitors and guests may contact number +918717357242, Email Id: snehakunj@gmail.com for advance booking. We have separate accommodation for patients and guests.

    Diet Center:

    Snehakunja has a well furnished modern pure vegetarian food court BLUE BAY CAFÉ located close to Kasarkod Eco-beach and Blue Flag Beach which caters meals, salads, soups, South Indian snacks, chaats, sandwiches, fruit juice, ice creams and beverages. The Diet Center also serves diet food based on the recommendations of the doctors/dieticians or self reported healthy diet foods.

    Conference Hall

    Snehakunja has a well-furnished conference hall (Dr. Kusuma Sabha Bhavana) and audiovisual services which could accommodate 50 people to attend workshops, meetings and community group consultations. There is open space adjacent to the hall to arrange catering services.

    Farm and Agro-Tourism services at Kavalagere

    Snehakunja has a well-furnished cottage at its Kavalagere farm 15 kms from Honnavar town on NH 66 of the foothills of Western Ghats. The farm has medicinal and horticulture plantation and a dairy with 15 locally bred traditional cows. Medicinal plants such as Turmeric, Sarsaparilla (Sogade Beru), Adatoda Vasica (Adusoge plant), Kirata Kaddi, Netmugs, Sarpagandha, Neem Tree, Butea Monosperma (Muttuga), Hog Plum Tree (Amatekayi), Ashoki, Ankole tree, Mimosa Pudica (Nachike mullu), Amla tree and many other.

    Harticultural plants such as Coconut, Arecanut and Banana etc

    The place is suitable for tourists and visitors to enjoy Western-Ghats nature, farm work and comfortable stay with contemporary food and room services.
