Nearby Attractions

Home > Nearby Attractions





Udupi

Krishna Matha

Famous for its Krishna Temple, unique temple architecture, vibrant religious activities.



Malpe

Malpe Beach

One of the most popular beaches in Udupi, Malpe Beach is known
for its golden sand, water sports activities, and the ferry service to
St. Mary's Island.



Kateel

Kateel Durga Parameshwari Temple

Located on an islet in the Nandini River, dedicated to Goddess Durga Parameshwari, scenic surroundings.






























Kaup

Kaup Beach

Famous for its historic lighthouse and scenic views,
Kaup Beach is a great spot for photography and
enjoying the coastal beauty.



Kadrie

Kadri Manjunath Temple

Ancient temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, stunning bronze statues,
serene surroundings.



Udupi

Delta Beach (Kodi Beach)

Known for its tranquility and the Kodi Bengre fishing village, it's a great place to experience local culture and enjoy fresh seafood.






























Kudroli

Kudroli Gokarnath Temple

Modern temple dedicated to Lord Gokarnath,
vibrant Navaratri celebrations, intricate carvings.



Panambur

Panambur Beach

Known for its cleanliness and well-maintained surroundings,
Panambur Beach is popular for water sports, kite festivals, and its
picturesque sunsets.



Someshwara

Someshwara Beach

Famous for its golden sand and large rocks called "Rudra Shile," it's a less crowded beach perfect for a peaceful retreat.
